WHO ARE WE?

Gentle Doggies is a decentralized NFT Project where we combine community growth and values, gaming, with a passionate initiative for dogs (owned and stray), their welfare, shelter and relationship with mankind to bring forth an innovative ecosystem for each dog and its owner.
If you own a Gentle Doggie NFT collectible, you get access to massive discounts and free items for your dogs, ranging from health care, feeding, dog accessories, special events and more.
Holders of the NFT will be gifted free $GDT (Gentle doggie tokens), your Doggie NFT will be used as a character for the play-to-earn Doggie Arcade.
The Gentle Doggie stands out from the regular NFTs so far, as they each have strong community value.

WHY GENTLE DOGGIES?

We strongly advocate for the wellbeing and catering of dogs, with a special focus on stray dogs. The wellbeing of every dog is paramount – owned or stray. This project is born out of the current level of care these beautiful creatures and the growing endemic of stray dogs plus the apathy and ill-treatment met by these strays.

To reach this goal we will be giving out 20% of total sales to partner agencies in the following niche:
 • Vet Hospitals
• Dog accessories agencies
• Dog Feed producers and retailers
• Influencers with similar-goals and objectives
 5% of total sales will be distributed to each niche mentioned above summing up to 20%

All proceeds will be utilized for the provision of health care, food, accessories and awareness for stray dogs and dogs owned by our NFT holders. This project is set to enormously cater and provide shelter for 70% of stray dogs in major continents of the world one dog at a time.
